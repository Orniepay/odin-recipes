Easy No-Churn Ice Cream

This easy no-churn ice cream recipe requires no special equipment
and can be customized with your favorite flavors.

This creamy and delicious homemade ice cream can be made
without an ice cream maker in just a few simple steps.

How to Make Easy No-Churn Ice Cream?

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below - but here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make no-churn ice cream:

  1. Whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Fold in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. Add any mix-ins or flavorings you desire.
  4. Pour into a freezer-safe container and freeze until firm.
  5. Scoop and enjoy your homemade ice cream!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional mix-ins (chocolate chips, fruit, nuts, etc.)

Directions:

Step 1:

Gather all ingredients.






Step 2:

In a large bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form,
about 3 minutes.





Step 3:

Gently fold in sweetened condensed milk and vanilla
until well combined.



Step 4:

If desired, fold in any additional mix-ins or flavorings.





Step 5:

Transfer mixture to a 9x5-inch loaf pan or other
freezer-safe container.



Step 6:

Cover tightly with plastic wrap or lid and freeze
until firm, at least 6 hours or overnight.





Step 7:

Scoop and serve. Enjoy your homemade ice cream!
